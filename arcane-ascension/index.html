Claim the map, hex by hex

Arcane Ascension is a turn-based strategy game where every hex you hold feeds your mana — and mana is everything. Summon wizards, capture territory, and manage your economy carefully: every wizard costs upkeep each turn, and an empire that expands too fast will starve. Push into rival kingdoms, cut their provinces in two, and seize their castles before they do the same to you.

Screenshots

⬡ Choose your faction

Five factions, five ways to fight — the balanced Arcanum, the fiery Emberkin Legion, the stalwart Stoneroot Wardens, the scheming Veilborn Court, and the fungal druids of the Sporewald Circle, who farm the blight itself. Each brings its own innate spells and its own citadel on the map.

✦ Ascend the wizard ladder

Four tiers of power. Higher tiers win battles but cost far more to keep — timing your ascensions is the heart of the game.

✴ Master the spellbook

Freeze an enemy, shield an ally, raise a stone wall, chain lightning through their ranks, seed a rival kingdom with creeping fungus, or seize control of an enemy wizard. Draft your loadout before each match.

♔ Three ways to win

Skirmish (classic conquest), King of the Citadel (hold the crowned hex), and Survival (outlast the escalating horde).

♜ Climb the campaign

A 120-level campaign across five ranks — escalating rivals, fog of war, asymmetric starts, and maps already overgrown with fungus.

☀ A new puzzle every day

The Daily Challenge deals everyone the same seeded map each day, rotating game modes through the week. Build your streak to earn milestone rewards.

⚒ Make it yours

Local pass-and-play for up to four players, spectator mode, fog of war, and a full sandbox: build scenarios and share them as text codes that work on every platform.

Fair and offline-friendly

No account. No forced ads. No pay-to-win. Matches work entirely offline; rewarded ads exist only as an optional choice, and you can turn off anonymous usage analytics in settings at any time.
