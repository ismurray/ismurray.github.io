About Me

I'm a tenacious developer with a background in biochemical research and a passion for solving tough problems.


I had my first introduction to software development through a Bioinformatics course I took while studying Biochemistry at Boston University. It was just a taste of Python, but I was immediately hooked. Since then, I’ve had a chance to build applications for biochemical research, ecommerce, and restaurant point of sale.


My experience in research instilled in me a relentless and detail-oriented approach to problem solving. In fact, some of my favorite moments in the development process come from hitting a wall. There’s a unique sense of satisfaction I get from pulling out a hammer and some nails to start building a ladder. I’m eager to bring my determination, and my passion for learning to a forward-thinking engineering team where I can help to drive growth through thoughtful, scalable solutions.
